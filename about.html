Meet Your Guru

A journey from student to scholar, dedicated to the ancient science of Yoga.

Academic Excellence & Tradition

Harshwardhan Singh Parmar is not just a yoga teacher; he is a scholar of Yogic Science. His journey began with a deep curiosity about the human potential, leading him to Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya (DAVV), Indore.

He completed his Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Yogic Science, laying a strong foundation in anatomy, physiology, and traditional texts. He continued his pursuit of knowledge with a Master of Science (M.Sc.) in Yoga, mastering advanced techniques and therapeutic applications.

Currently, he is a PhD Research Scholar at DAVV, contributing to the scientific understanding of Yoga. He also serves as a faculty member, teaching the next generation of yoga instructors at the university.

Experience That Matters

With over 5-6 years of rigorous theoretical study and practical teaching, Harshwardhan brings a unique blend of academic precision and intuitive healing to his classes. He specializes in personalized home visits, ensuring that every session is tailored to the individual's body type, ailments, and goals.
